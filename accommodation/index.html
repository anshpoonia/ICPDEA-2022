Note

All the speakers and invited guests will be accommodated in Fortune Park Orange, Sidhrawali (at a walking distance of 2 minutes from the BMU main entrance). Other participants including post-doctoral fellows, research scholars and students will be accommodated inside BMU campus.

Fortune Park Orange

Fortune Park Orange, Sidhrawali, Gurgaon is an upscale, contemporary full service hotel with a strategic location off National Highway 8. Its convenient location provides easy access to the business hub of Gurgaon and the industrial belts of Bhiwadi, Bawal and Manesar. A 45 minute drive from Indira Gandhi International Airport on the Delhi-Jaipur Highway, Fortune Park Orange is surrounded by lush green sprawling lawns and open spaces.

Visa Information

Citizens of most countries will require a valid travel document (passport) and a valid visa for issued by the Indian embassy or the Indian consulate around the world. For the invited participants in this conference from outside India, it would perhaps be most appropriate to obtain a Conference Visa. The procedure for obtaining a Conference visa have been simplified by the Govt. of India in recent past and an official letter of invitation signed by the Indian organizer should suffice for obtaining a conference visa.
