About the conference

In order to strengthen cooperation and exchanges in the field of mathematics among the five countries, Brazil, China, India, Russia and South Africa, the BRICS Mathematics Conference was launched in 2016.

The fourth conference in this series will be held in India at IISER Thiruvananthapuram from December 07-10, 2021. This conference will feature 15 plenary talks, 3 from each of the five BRICS nations, and several contributed talks and posters. Participation from anyone interested in mathematics or statistics is welcome. Registration is now open and can be done here. Click on the buttons at the top of this page for more information. Further details will be provided in the days to come.
