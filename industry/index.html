Details
A special Industry Session is planned during the conference to listen and understand problems faced by industry experts in their respective domains and possible solutions using advanced computational and mathematical algorithms with particular focus on problems in Computer & IT industry, Electrical & Electronics Engineering, Mechanical & Structural Engineering, Oil & Gas, Materials & Metallurgical Engineering and Space industry.

It is expected to cover applications and relevance of Partial Differential Equations & Numerical Algorithms in the following industries:

    1. Mechanical/ Automobile/ Manufacturing
      • Challenges in electric vehicles design and development
      • Vibrations
      • Heat transfer
      • Acoustics
      • Fluid flows/ two-phase flows
      • Elasticity problems
      • Modelling/simulation of solid and structural mechanical problems
    2. Electrical/ Electronics/Telecom
      • Electrical networks
      • Signal processing
      • Transmission lines
      • Electrostatistics & electrodynamics
    3. Oil and gas industry
      • Oil and gas dynamics
      • Flow in reservoirs including simulations
      • Modelling of petroleum extraction
    4. Aerospace
      • Aero-dynamics
      • Propulsion
      • Structural dynamics
  2. Expectation from the Industry Delegates

    We expect industry participants to provide problems which they are facing, particularly those that can be answered in the framework of mathematical and computational algorithms, partial differential equations (PDEs), mathematical simulation software and scientific computing. The industry participants need not to provide the problem as a mathematical model but a broad idea of the challenges they are facing so that the experts (from India and abroad) can understand and try to provide an answer using a robust mathematical model, numerical algorithms using PDEs.

  3. Academic Institutions participating? (Student delegates coming from)

    Postgraduate students, research scholars and early career researchers from IITs/NITs/Central Universities, private universities, private colleges from India and from abroad (Austria, Germany, UK, Scotland, Korea, Hongkong, KSA) are participating in the conference. A final list of confirmed participants will be updated on the conference website 10 days before the start of the conference.

  4. Media coverage planned for the event

    The conference proceedings (daily updates, videos and live telecast) will be available on the official social media pages (LinkedIn, Facebook & Youtube) of BMU. Daily updates will also be sent to local newspapers for print media coverage.

  5. Specific topics which will be covered during the Conference

    The conference speakers (plenary, invited and contributed talks) will broadly cover topics ranging in the interdisciplinary field of Partial Differential Equations, Numerical Algorithms, Scientific computing which have vast applications and solutions to problems in Mechanical/Structural, Aerospace Engineering, Software & IT, Weather Forecasting, Environmental Engineering, Fluid Flows etc.
